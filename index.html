Roadmap

Phase 1

The Foodles Club is drawn on the Ethereum blockchain and they begin their journey! An unique collection of 5000 NFTs. The first 700 mints will be free.

Phase 2

At 20% minted, we will airdrop 5 Foodles Club to random holders. Additionally, the will community will vote to select which charity we donate to.

Phase 3

At 50% minted, we will auction off a unique 1/1 Foodle. All proceeds will be donated to a charity supporting the diversity and inclusion of women, chosen by our community.

Phase 4

At 100% minted, we will develop a creative incubator to empower female creators and artists. Aspiring female artists will be able to elevate their platform and we will act as a safe space and advocate for emerging creators. Our community will be able to choose which projects we fund and help with development. We will assist them by providing marketing and guidance.

FAQ

What are Foodles?

The Foodles Club (FC) is a collection of 5000 unique NFTs. We are a community-driven project aiming to develop a creative incubator to empower female creators and artists. We are not associated in any way from any projects we derive from.

What about Doodles?

All of our art is handcrafted and drawn from scratch. We have unique traits. We're inspired by the Sanrio-art style, popularized in Japan. Foodles Club is not affiliated with Doodles LLC in any way.
